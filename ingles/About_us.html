OUR FIRM

Consultores Jurídicos is a law firm that brings together the experience of several professionals that have successfully evolved in areas of constitutional, public, private and international law. We have an entrepreneur and dynamic team with an outstanding trajectory, leaded by attorneys Carlos Ayala Corao, Desmond Dillon Mc Loughlin, Gerardo Fernández Villegas, Rafael Chavero Gazdik y José Annicchiarico Villagrán.

The firm is set up by areas of expertise, coordinated by one or more partners specialized in the subject, who lead the rest of the team that integrate it. We offer a team with academic soundness, professional development in the areas of negotiation and arbitration, litigation in national and international courts, and important networks with the corporate and institutional environment in business, regulatory, banking, insurance and telecommunication areas. Consultores Jurídicos is organized and prepared to provide rigorous and timely advice to our clients.

Some of its members are prominent professors of undergraduate and graduate school in the most prestigious universities here and abroad, have worked in international bodies, firms and companies in the United States, and are prepared to attend the client’s needs in different languages.

As a part of their professional exercise, the lawyers at this law firm, individually and collectively, dedicate part of their time pro bono to matters of social interest, as a sincere expression of commitment to the people that require legal assistance, but do not have the economic means.

Since 2007, the firm is a member of the Pro Bono Declaration for the Americas, an organization that gathers the most prestigious firms of the United States and the rest of the continent, that are committed to those in need.
